3 Iconic cocktails

Old Fashioned
ingredients

2 oz of bourbon, 1/2 oz of simple syrup and 4 dashes of aromatic bitters. Add ingredients double rocks over ice block and stir gently.

Southside
ingredients

2 oz of gin, 1 oz of simple syrup, 1 oz of lime juice and 4 mint leaves. Pour ingredients into shaker with ice, shake then double strain into coupe.

Mai Tai (easy version)
ingredients

2 oz of aged rum, 1/2 oz of orange curacao, 1/2 oz of orgeat and 1 oz of lime juice. Pour into shaker strain into glass with ice.
